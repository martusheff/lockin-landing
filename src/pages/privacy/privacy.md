# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 3/8/2026

LockIn is committed to protecting your privacy. LockIn is designed to work with minimal data collection and without account creation.

## Information We Collect

LockIn does not store your personal data on our servers.

- **Photos and progress data** are stored locally on your device only
- Photos may be saved in both app storage and your camera roll/gallery
- **No personal photos or progress content** are transmitted to our servers
- We may collect **anonymous usage analytics** and **crash reports** to improve reliability and performance

## Photo Storage and Security

LockIn stores photos locally in:

- The app's local/private storage
- Your device's camera roll/gallery

**Important:** We are not liable for unauthorized access, loss, or leakage of photos stored on your device. Because we do not control your device storage or camera roll, data protection and backups are your responsibility.

## User Responsibility

Because LockIn has no cloud account or remote backup system:

- You are solely responsible for backing up your photos
- If you uninstall the app, photos in app storage may be permanently lost
- Photos already in your camera roll typically remain after uninstall

## Third-Party Services

LockIn may use third-party analytics or crash reporting providers. Those providers operate under their own privacy policies.
